Installation

Start by adding the Kilt gem to your Gemfile:

                gem 'kilt-cms'

Then bundle your gems:

                bundle install

Finally, in order to auto-load Kilt's dependencies, you must require Kilt in your application.rb:

                module Blah
                  class Application < Rails::Application
                    require 'kilt'
                  end
                end
